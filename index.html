Bio

I received my PhD in Computer Science in 2017 from the National University of Singapore (NUS). I worked as a research fellow at the NUS Natural Language Processing (NLP) Group from 2017 to 2019 with Prof. NG Hwee Tou, who was my PhD advisor. Currently, I am working as a software engineer at the Shopee DEEP Team.

My research interests include machine translation (MT), natural language processing (NLP), and artificial intelligence.
